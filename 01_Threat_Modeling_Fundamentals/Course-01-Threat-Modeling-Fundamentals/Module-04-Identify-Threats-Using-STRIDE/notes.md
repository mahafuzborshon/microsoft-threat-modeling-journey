- Threat modeling requires a clear focus

- Two main approaches:

  - Asset-focused:
    - What needs protection
    - Focus on sensitive data and systems

  - Threat-focused:
    - Who is the attacker
    - Focus on attacker behavior

- System-focused approach:
  - Analyze entire system structure

- Importance of choosing focus:
  - Avoid confusion
  - Improve efficiency
  - Better threat identification

- Key idea:
  - Decide focus before starting analysis
