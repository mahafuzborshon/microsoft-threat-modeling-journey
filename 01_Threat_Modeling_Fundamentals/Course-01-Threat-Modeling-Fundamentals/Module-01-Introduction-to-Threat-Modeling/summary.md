In this module, I learned the basics of threat modeling and why it's important in cybersecurity. It helped me understand how security should be considered from the design stage, not after building a system.

I explored the overall phases of threat modeling and saw a simple step-by-step approach: design, break, fix, and verify. This made the process feel structured and practical.

The “design” step showed me how to understand the system first. Then in “break”, I learned how attackers might find weaknesses. “Fix” focused on improving security, and finally “verify” ensured everything works as expected.

Overall, this module gave me a clear mindset: think like both a builder and an attacker.
